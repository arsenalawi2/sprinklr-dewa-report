AI-Driven Transformation of Community Management via Sprinklr

Leveraging Sprinklr's Full Platform to Automate, Integrate, and Enhance DEWA's Social-to-CRM Workflow

Prepared for: EVP & CAIO I&TF

Prepared by: Hadi Abou Chaaban

Date: April 14, 2025

Executive Summary

DEWA's current social media workflow—Sprinklr Social monitoring ➝ Excel ➝ Email ➝ SAP CRM—is manual, fragmented, and slow. Critical time is lost in transferring data and managing customer issues. We propose activating Sprinklr's full AI-powered suite—Sprinklr Service, Insights, AI+, and CRM integration—to automate triage, enhance agent productivity, eliminate the Excel/email dependency, and create seamless SAP CRM case creation. This aligns with DEWA's strategic goals of digital transformation, operational excellence, and world-class customer experience.
