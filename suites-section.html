Sprinklr Product Suites

Sprinklr offers a comprehensive set of product suites that work together to provide an integrated customer experience management platform. Below is a detailed overview of each suite and its products:

Suite Product Description
Sprinklr Service Sprinklr Voice An AI-powered Contact Center as a Service (CCaaS) solution that enables digital-first customer care across 30+ channels, integrating voice bots and real-time insights.
Self-Service Community Allows customers to share knowledge in customizable digital communities, reducing agent caseloads and enhancing cross-platform customer experiences.
Live Chat Provides real-time, personalized 1:1 support through chat, enabling quick resolution of customer inquiries and data collection for improved service.
Conversational AI & Bots Utilizes AI-powered chatbots and virtual assistants to engage in natural, human-like conversations, offering instant responses and resolving routine queries.
Contact Center Intelligence Offers AI-driven insights into contact center operations, enhancing decision-making and operational efficiency.
Contact Center Automation Automates routine contact center tasks, improving agent productivity and reducing operational costs.
Agent Assist Provides agents with real-time guidance, suggested responses, and next-best actions to enhance customer interactions.
Knowledge Base Centralized repository of information that supports both customers and agents with accurate, up-to-date content.
Customer Service Delivers consistent and efficient customer service across various channels, integrating AI and automation to enhance the customer experience.
Sprinklr Insights Social Listening Monitors social media channels to gather insights on brand perception, customer sentiment, and emerging trends.
Competitive Insights and Benchmarking Analyzes competitors' performance and benchmarks against industry standards to inform strategic decisions.
Product Insights Tracks customer feedback and discussions related to specific products, aiding in product development and improvement.
Location Insights Provides geo-specific data to understand regional performance and customer behavior.
Visual Insights Utilizes AI to analyze visual content across platforms, offering insights into brand visibility and perception.
Media Monitoring & Analytics Tracks and analyzes media coverage across channels, helping manage brand reputation and PR strategies.
Sprinklr Social Social Publishing & Engagement Manages and schedules social media posts across platforms, facilitating audience engagement and content distribution.
Distributed Enables local teams to manage social media activities while maintaining brand consistency across regions.
Conversational Commerce Integrates shopping experiences into social conversations, allowing customers to make purchases directly through social platforms.
Ratings & Review Monitors and manages customer reviews and ratings across platforms, aiding in reputation management.
Local Social Supports localized social media strategies, tailoring content and engagement to specific regions or communities.
Sprinklr Marketing Campaign Planning & Content Marketing Streamlines campaign management and content creation across channels, reducing production costs and accelerating launches.
Marketing Analytics Provides AI-powered analytics to measure and optimize marketing performance across digital and social channels.
Advocacy Empowers employees and partners to share branded content, extending reach and enhancing brand credibility.
Social Advertising Centralizes multichannel social advertising efforts, utilizing AI for optimization and comprehensive reporting.
Sprinklr Digital Twin Digital Twin An AI-powered solution that creates autonomous and intelligent replicas of your brand, teams, or employees. These digital counterparts can engage in conversations, make decisions, design workflows, and execute tasks at scale, enhancing customer service and operational efficiency.
Unified Platform Sprinklr AI Sprinklr's proprietary AI engine that powers the platform's core capabilities, delivering high-accuracy insights and automation across customer experience functions.

AI Studio A workspace for building, training, and deploying custom AI models tailored to specific business needs, enabling advanced automation and insights.
Security Provides enterprise-grade security features, including data privacy, access controls, and compliance tools to protect sensitive information and ensure regulatory adherence.
Integrations Facilitates seamless integration with existing software systems, unifying data and enhancing collaboration across various tools and platforms.
Sandbox Offers a testing environment to experiment with configurations and workflows without affecting live operations, allowing for safe development and testing.
Display Transforms data into dynamic visualizations for digital signage, enhancing real-time information sharing and decision-making.
Presentations Enables the creation of dynamic presentations that integrate live data, facilitating impactful storytelling and reporting.
